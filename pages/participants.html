Evaluation Criteria

How Your Team Will Be Scored

Final score = Pitching & Prototype (80%) + TikTok Reflection (20%) = 100 marks total. Know what judges are looking for so you can focus your effort in the right places.

80%
Pitching & Prototype
8 criteria × 5 pts = 40 raw → /80
  • PT1 Clear problem statement and user need
  • PT2 Evidence of Design Thinking process across the 5 phases
  • PT3 Cultural heritage integration
  • PT4 Innovation and creativity of the solution
  • PT5 Prototype clarity, function and relevance
  • PT6 Testing feedback and improvement actions
  • PT7 Presentation flow, storytelling and teamwork
  • PT8 Response during Q&A
20%
TikTok Reflection
5 criteria × 5 pts = 25 raw → /20
  • TT1 Shows clear learning journey from Day 1 to Day 4
  • TT2 Demonstrates teamwork and group involvement
  • TT3 Explains Design Thinking process, problem, idea or prototype clearly
  • TT4 Connects with cultural heritage / CAMP21 theme
  • TT5 Creativity, clarity and audience engagement

🏆 Best Speaker — Separate Nomination
Judged independently by each judge team. Does not affect your group's final score. Each judge team (A & B) nominates 1 Best Speaker from their 5 groups. Scored on clarity, confidence, explanation, Q&A response, and overall impact.
