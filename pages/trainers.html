Trainer SOP

Simple operating procedure to keep all trainer support consistent across 10 groups.

Before Input

Prepare the phase

Confirm the phase objective, required templates, timing, lead/support roles and Smart DT App links before the session starts.

During Input

Teach, guide, question

Deliver concise instructions, model one example, then circulate to support teams with guiding questions instead of giving direct solutions.

After Input

Verify output

Check that the minimum template outputs are completed and flag any team needing facilitator follow-up or extra support.
