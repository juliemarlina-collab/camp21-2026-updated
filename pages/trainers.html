⚖️ Quality Assurance Protocol

Accompanying Lecturers (Pensyarah Pengiring) Scope

Unbiased Assistance
Serve strictly as unbiased, rotating facilitators across all 10 student teams during phase checkpoints and pedagogical transitions.
⚖️
The Rotation Rule
Explicitly NOT assigned to support your own polytechnic's team. Faculty must rotate to a brand new group at every major checkpoint to protect assessment impartiality and offer diverse critique vectors.
🔄
Checkpoint Sign-Off
Oversee physical milestone gates and verify that teams have logged their current deliverables before signing them off to advance to the next phase.
📝
Platform Monitoring
Track live cohort submissions via the **Trainer View** inside the digital hub terminal, instantly flagging overdue canvases or delayed uploads to the Master Trainer.
